Salad

Salads are a great way to add a variety of fruits and vegetables to your diet, which can help lower your risk of stroke.

Chicken Rice

Chicken rice is a healthy and delicious dish that can help prevent stroke. Chicken is a good source of protein, while rice provides energy and essential nutrients. Together, they make a well-balanced meal that can help maintain healthy blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Fish Soup

Fish Soup is a healthy dish that is rich in Omega-3 fatty acids, which have been shown to reduce the risk of stroke. It is also a low-calorie, high-protein meal that can help maintain a healthy weight and promote heart health.
