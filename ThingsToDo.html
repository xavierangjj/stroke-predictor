Book a Badminton Court

Badminton is a great way to improve cardiovascular health, which can help reduce the risk of stroke. This sport also helps improve hand-eye coordination, balance, and agility, all of which are important for maintaining overall physical health. Additionally, playing badminton can reduce stress and anxiety, promoting mental well-being.

Join a Yoga Class

Yoga is a gentle form of exercise that can improve cardiovascular health, reduce stress, and lower blood pressure. These benefits make it a great option for preventing stroke. Additionally, practicing yoga regularly can improve flexibility, balance, and overall physical fitness, which are all important factors in maintaining good health and reducing the risk of stroke.

Join a Tai Chi Session

Tai chi is a low-impact exercise that promotes balance, flexibility, and relaxation. Regular practice of tai chi has been associated with a reduced risk of stroke by improving cardiovascular health, reducing stress, and improving blood pressure and cholesterol levels. Additionally, it can improve physical and mental well-being, making it a great way to stay active and healthy.
